About Porsche

History

Porsche is a renowned German automobile manufacturer known for its high-performance sports cars, sedans, and SUVs. Founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931, the company has a rich history deeply rooted in automotive engineering and motorsport.

Mission

Porsche's mission is to produce high-performance, premium vehicles that combine cutting-edge technology, exquisite design, and driving pleasure.

Notable Achievements

  • Porsche has achieved great success in motorsport, with numerous victories in prestigious events such as the 24 Hours of Le Mans, the 24 Hours of Daytona, and the World Endurance Championship (WEC).
  • The Porsche 911, introduced in 1963, has become an automotive icon and is one of the most recognizable sports cars in the world.
